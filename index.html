What is Native Strength? Native Strength is a centuries-old inter-tribal traditional mental health care system. It uses medicine wheels, teaching triangles, and traditional stories to illustrate concepts and provide useful tools for personal insight and coping strategies.

Her Impressive Background!

In 2001 Hyapatia Lee was elected to the prestigious position of Blessed Woman for the Lost River band of the Cherokee. Every weekend she taught the people to speak Cherokee, conducted pipe ceremonies and presided over sweat lodges, healing ceremonies. As she began to teach the many things she had learned from the elders, it became apparent that this information was just as useful to the members of the Lost River Band as it had been to her.
